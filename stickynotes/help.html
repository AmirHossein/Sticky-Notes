Help...
  • I lost my note and can't find it after editing!
    There is a structure to sort notes:
    • Deleting notes always sends them to Trash!
    • Archived notes moves to Archived UNLESS they're deleted.
    • Finished notes moves to Finished UNLESS they're deleted or archived.
    • Assigning notes to other users sends notes to ToDo panel UNLESS they're deleted or archived.
    • Other notes stay on left side panel.
    If you still have problem, use search box at the bottom of page. You could search by 'Title' and 'Comment'.
  • I've deleted a note and its placed in Trash. How can i restore it?
    Try to Edit deleted note. You can see a new checkbox "Restore" in the form. Check it and Save.
  • Some sections refreshes automatically on startup. What about others?
    When you click on a section title e.g. "ToDo list" or "Finished Tasks" and etc, the section collapses and a Cookie is created. The collapsed sections will NOT refresh in next start automatically. So you can prevent refreshing sections you like by collapssing them.
